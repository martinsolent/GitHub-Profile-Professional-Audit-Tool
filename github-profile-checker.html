✍️ Crafted Bio / About Section
Does the profile include a clear bio: who they are, what they study/build, and maybe a personal touch?
📊 Recent Commit Activity
Is there visible activity on the contribution graph? Recent commits show an active, engaged developer.
📌 Pinned Showcase Repositories
Are there pinned repos that highlight best work and key skills? This is your digital portfolio shop window.
🏷️ Repo Descriptions
Do the repositories have short, clear descriptions? Blank descriptions look unfinished and unprofessional.
📄 Documented READMEs
Do key repos have a proper README? Should include: what it is, how to run it, and ideally screenshots or a demo.
